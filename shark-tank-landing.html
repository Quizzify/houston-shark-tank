Winners select either a $25,000 cash investment (pari passu, cramdown-protected) or $25,000 in Quizzify/Validation Institute services — fully customizable and transferable.

Each contestant budgets $100 to award to the audience member whose comment they find most insightful. This incentivizes high-quality, actionable feedback.
